Addendum for the CSS:

1.Font Choices:
- For body text, 'Segoe UI' was chosen as the primary font. It's a clean and modern sans-serif font commonly found on Windows systems, offering good readability.
- For headings, the same font ('Segoe UI') was chosen for consistency, with increased font-weight for emphasis. This maintains a cohesive visual identity throughout the document.
2.Sizes of Headings and Main Text:
- Headings are sized proportionally according to their importance, with larger headings (h1) for main titles and smaller headings (h6) for subheadings, ensuring clear hierarchy and readability.
- The main text size (paragraphs, links, lists) is set to 16px for comfortable reading without being too large or small.
3.Color Choices:
- Blue tones were chosen for headings and links to create visual hierarchy and consistency.
- #007bff was selected as the primary color for headings to make them stand out while maintaining a professional look.
- The same color (#007bff) was used for links to ensure they are easily recognizable and clickable.
- White (#fff) was chosen for footer text to provide sufficient contrast against the background color.
4.Margin and Padding Choices:
- Padding around the whole document ensures content is spaced nicely from the edges, improving readability and aesthetics.
- Margin between sections creates visual separation, making it easier for users to distinguish different content areas.
- Padding for headings and text within sections provides breathing space, enhancing readability and visual appeal.
- Padding for the footer separates it from the rest of the content, ensuring it doesn't feel cramped and maintaining overall balance in the design.
